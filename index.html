Real-World Case Studies

Learn from actual security incidents and understand how to prevent similar attacks.

Case Study: Identity Theft Through Social Media

The Incident

A user posted birthday celebration photos with visible personal details including date of birth and location metadata.

The Vulnerability

Attackers aggregated publicly available information to answer security questions and gain account access.

Prevention

Limit personal information in public posts. Use non-obvious security questions. Enable 2FA on all accounts.

Case Study: Man-in-the-Middle Attack on Public Wi-Fi

The Incident

User connected to an unsecured public Wi-Fi network and accessed banking services without VPN protection.

The Vulnerability

Malicious actor intercepted unencrypted traffic, capturing login credentials and session tokens.

Prevention

Always use VPN on public networks. Avoid sensitive transactions. Verify network legitimacy with venue staff.

Case Study: Spear Phishing Through Recruitment Fraud

The Incident

Victim received a convincing job offer email requesting personal documents and financial information for "onboarding."

The Vulnerability

Fraudsters used stolen data to open fraudulent accounts and commit identity fraud, causing financial loss.

Prevention

Verify offers through official channels. Never share SSN/Aadhaar via email. Research companies thoroughly before engagement.
